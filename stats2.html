Overview

Around 348 million people in the Arab region are online, representing about 70% of the area's 496 million inhabitants. This high level of Internet and social media penetration has transformed consumer habits and pushed both governments and businesses to develop advanced digital strategies that support the digital economy and encourage long-term investment growth.
